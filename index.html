SEGRA-SWEEPER

Step 1

COLLECT!

The Segra-sweeper starts by collecting your waste.

This eliminates the need for workers to go through toxic materials, that are very harmful!

Step 2

SEGREGATE

The Segra-sweeper automatically segrates your waste into three categories:

DRY WASTE, WET WASTE, and E-WASTE.

Step 3

REDUCE. REUSE. RECYCLE.

We collect your waste and recycle and reuse whatever we can!

Step 4

Save the world, one step at a time

By stopping waste from entering landfills and oceans, we are able to save the planet,

One Step at a Time.
